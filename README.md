### About

I build production-grade SaaS, AI agent systems, and modern web/mobile applications for startups and product teams.

My work is focused on taking ideas from messy requirements to reliable software: clean architecture, scalable databases, background jobs, payment flows, AI workflows, and polished product interfaces.

I mostly work with **Ruby on Rails**, **React/TypeScript**, **Flutter**, **PostgreSQL**, **Redis**, **Docker**, and modern AI tooling.
